Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Initial Investment Cost in dollars.
  2. Enter the Net Gain or Return from the investment in dollars.
  3. Click the Calculate button to determine the Return on Investment (ROI).

Formula:

ROI (%) = [(Net Gain or Return - Initial Investment Cost) / Initial Investment Cost] * 100%
