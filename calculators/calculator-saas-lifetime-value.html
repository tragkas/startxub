Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Average Revenue Per User (ARPU).
  2. Enter the Gross Margin (as a percentage).
  3. Enter the Churn Rate (as a percentage).
  4. Click the Calculate button to determine the SaaS Lifetime Value (LTV).

Formula:

SaaS LTV = (ARPU * Gross Margin) / Churn Rate
