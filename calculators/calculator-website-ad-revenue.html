Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Website Traffic (number of visitors).
  2. Enter the Click-Through Rate (CTR) as a percentage (e.g., 3% as 3).
  3. Enter the Cost Per Click (CPC) in dollars.
  4. Click the Calculate button to estimate ad revenue.

Formula:

Ad Revenue = (Website Traffic * CTR / 100) * CPC
