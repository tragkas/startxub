Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Fixed Costs (e.g., rent, salaries).
  2. Enter the Variable Costs per unit (e.g., production costs).
  3. Enter the Selling Price per unit.
  4. Click the Calculate button to determine the number of units or revenue needed to break even.

Formula:

Break-even Units = Fixed Costs / (Selling Price per unit - Variable Costs per unit)

Break-even Revenue = Break-even Units * Selling Price per unit
