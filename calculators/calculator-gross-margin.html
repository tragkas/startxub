Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Total Revenue in dollars.
  2. Enter the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) in dollars.
  3. Click the Calculate button to determine the Gross Margin.

Formula:

Gross Margin (%) = ((Total Revenue - COGS) / Total Revenue) * 100%
